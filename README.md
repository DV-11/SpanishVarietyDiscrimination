# Folders

- **Association Task**: Notebooks used to conduct the Association Task.
- **Decision Task**: Notebooks Used to conduct the Decision Task.
- **Data**: Datasets for adjectives, job titles, Spanish sentences, etc.  
- **Errors & Failed Tasks**: Datasets of errors/hallucinations in model responses and tasks that were not completed properly.
- **Plots**: Visualisations included in the Master Thesis report
- **Response Processing**: Notebooks used to process the results 
- **Results**: Responses by the models from the Association and Decision Task, as well as the results from the statistical significance tests. 
- **Spanish LLM Test**: Notebooks and results from the testing of LLMs with a high volume of Spanish-language training data, which were not ultimately included in this study.
